I am a UX Researcher and Designer living in Denver.

I don’t have a background in design, but I’ve always enjoyed beautiful things. Calligraphy. Illustrations. Poetry. Furniture. Typography. The list goes on. I loved the dedication and thoughtfulness required to craft something that evoked an emotional response in people. I became interested in UX design because empathy plays such an important role in building an enjoyable experience. It’s very fulfilling to me to take the information that I’ve gathered through research and testing and use it to guide my design decisions.
